Let's share

This is a site for people to share common interest including movies, books, music.

Movies:
You have My movie part which includes two specific group: to_watch list and watched list, you can gather movies into each group according to youself.
You also can comment on movies and see others' comments and enjoy communications.

Group:
You can also create or join a group created by others, and post topics on that group. You will see all the new topics in your groups.

For the Books and Music, we leave it unconstructed for furthermore work.

After signup or signin, user logs into the homepage by default.
You can switch different tags to see contents of movies, groups, etc.
